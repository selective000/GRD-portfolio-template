Trompe L'Oiel Poster 

  • Promotional Material
  • Design & Layout
  • Illustration
  • Digital Painting

The Trompe L'Oiel poster came about for a project in my History of Graphic Design class: 

The goal of this project was two-fold; first to learn about a prominent influential artist from history, and second to be able to sucessfully identify the techniques and recreate said artist's style of artwork in a way that was both true to the Artist's vision and aesthetic. 

The poster was created by combining a base of Oil Pastel artwork enhanced through digital painting in photoshop, then finished by printing and depositing it into an antique picture frame.
