Projects

 

 

     Atelier Fashion Show Promotional Poster

  • Print Advertising
  • Design & Layout
  • Illustration

This poster is a part of a wider-scope project to advertise St Clair College's annual Aterlier Fashion Show, operated and ran bu the studeents of the Fashion Course. The theme for the year was Grunge/Alternative. The inspiration I pulled from originates in the 90's grunge aesthetic itself:fashion, makeup, culture, etc. Another prominent place I pulled inspiration from was the aesthetic and identites of several Metal & Rock Bands, such as Falling In Reverse, A Day To Remember, and Skrillex. The poster had to be bold, unique and eye-catching: the reds, blacks and grey colour pallet lends itself to that grungy, underground aesthetic they were looking for, and the illustration only adds to the alternative vibe.

     Local Funerary Card

  • Digital Artwork
  • Commission
  • Illustration

This Memorial Card was created for a commision of one of my acquaintences as part of an event package for a funeral that was taking place. The client requested a colour palette of pastel blues and pinks, with imagery of angelic figures and an abstract idea of what heavan could look like. With a gold-trim on the outside, this mockup was delivered to the client after several revisions.
